Adopt a cat

Now, you might be thinking: "I wish I could take one of those adorable cats home!". Well, dont fret, we're about to let you in on a little secret: You can! All you have to do is pick out a cat that struck your eye, contact us, and soon you'll have yourself a new fuzzy friend. All our cats are fully socialized, so the two of you will get along in no time. If you're unsure, you can always just make a reservation and come see us in person. Both the cats and humans here would love to meet you! Just make sure you take care of our little friends, okay?
